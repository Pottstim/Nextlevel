# Next Level Window Cleaning — Static Website

A fully responsive, single-page marketing website for a local window cleaning business serving Sanford, NC and the Sandhills region.

| Section | Status |
|---|---|
| Top info bar (hours + location) | ✅ |

| Hero section + floating review card | ✅ |
| NC trust / local knowledge section | ✅ |
| Services grid (4 cards) | ✅ |

---

## 🔧 Tech Stack

- **HTML5** — semantic markup throughout
- **Tailwind CSS** (CDN) — utility-first styling
- **Lucide Icons** (CDN) — lightweight SVG icons
- **Google Fonts** — Inter typeface
- **Vanilla JS** — scroll effects, mobile menu, form validation

## 🚧 Not Yet Implemented (Recommended Next Steps)

1. **Real form backend** — Wire the contact form to Formspree, Netlify Forms, or EmailJS so messages actually reach the business owner
2. **Real photography** — Replace all `[ placeholder ]` sections with real photos of the owner, team, truck, or completed jobs
3. **Google Reviews embed** — Pull live star ratings from Google Business Profile
4. **Google Maps embed** — Service area map in the contact section
5. **Analytics** — Add Google Analytics 4 or Plausible for traffic tracking
6. **Schema.org markup** — Add `LocalBusiness` JSON-LD for richer Google search results
7. **Testimonials carousel** — Rotate multiple customer reviews
8. **Before/After photo gallery** — Powerful trust-builder for cleaning businesses
9. **Pricing section** — Even ballpark ranges reduce phone inquiries and build trust
